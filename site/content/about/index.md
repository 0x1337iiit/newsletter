title: 'About the 0x1337 Initiative'
---

Welcome to **0xDispatch**, the official publication and intelligence feed of the **0x1337 Hacking Club**.

We are a collective of security researchers, hackers, and cyber security enthusiasts ased out of IIIT Hyderabad. We try to explore the depths of cybersecurity and share our findings with the broader community.

**Our Directives:**

- **Intelligence Reports** - Detailed write-ups on recent vulnerabilities, exploits, and security trends.
- **Operations & Projects** - Insights into our active research and open-source tooling.
- **CTF Transmissions** - Strategies and write-ups from our engagements in Capture The Flag competitions.
- **Technical Deep Dives** - Explorations of reverse engineering, cryptography, web security, and binary exploitation.

We operate under the principle that knowledge should be free and systems should be tested. This dispatch serves as our medium to transmit what we learn.

Stay curious. Stay secure.
